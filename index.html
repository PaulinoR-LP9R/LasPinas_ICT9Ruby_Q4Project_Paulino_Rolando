  • Mobile Suit Gundam: Hathaway

    Synopsis

    Hathaway Noa, now under the alias "Mufti Nabiyy Erin" joins a terrorist organization named "Mufti" to combat and fight against the abusive Earth Federation who plans to privatize the Earth for the privileged.

    Cast

    Kensho Ono, Reina Ueda, Jun'ichi Suwabe

    Director

    Hidekazu Hara, Ko Matsuo, Shuko Murase

    Duration

    1hr 35mins

    My Comments

    A wonderful anime movie that shows how the rich are always favored aside from the less fortunate. Much like the following entry (Iron Blooded Orphans) the world of Gundam has two drastically different people, the rich and the less fortunate. This movie shows the battle between the rich and the poor. If you are interested in movies that tackle socio-political issues this is just for you.

    8/10 A Great Movie




  • Mobile Suit Gundam: Iron Blooded Orphans

    Synopsis

    300 years after a cataclysmic war named the "Calamity War", a ragtag group of freedom fighters and child soldiers named "Tekkadan" with the help of Mobile Suits that served in the Calamity War challenge the government "Gjallarhorn" and answer the call for justice.

    Cast

    Kengo Kawanishi, Hosoya Yoshimasa, Yuka Terasaki, Takahiro Sakurai, Kousuke Toriumi, Masaya Matsukaze, Hisako Kanemoto

    Creators

    Yoshiyuki Tomino, Hajime Yatate

    Duration

    2 Seasons, 50 Episodes, 24mins each

    My Comments and Rating

    A wonderful anime and series that sheds light on the corruption that occurs in governments and the misfortune that happens to many people. Utilizing the child soldiers of Tekkadan, they portray the desperation of the less fortunate. This show does have a lot of bloody scenes.

    9.5/10 A Must Watch.




  • Jujutsu Kaisen: 0

    Synopsis

    The prequel to Jujutsu Kaisen (2020). Yuta Okkotsu is a young boy haunted by his childhood friend Rika who died in a traffic incident and transformed into a monstrous and powerful cursed spirit that will do anything to protect Yuta and he is unable to stop her from doing so. This catches the attention of Sorcerer Gojo Satoru and enrolls him in the Tokyo Prefectural Jujutsu High School where he learns to control Rika and possibly bring her peace.

    Cast

    Megumi Ogata, Tomokazu Seki, Koki Uchiyama, Mikako Komatsu, Kana Hanazawa,

    Director

    Seong-Hu Park

    Duration

    1hr 52mins

    My Comments

    A wonderful anime movie with a tale of reconcilation, lost love, and finding your place and role in the world. The story of the two childhood friends Yuta and Rika and Yuta's task of freeing Rika's soul from her form as a monster is touching. If you are interested in magic and the supernatural, this is your thing.

    8.9/10 Amazing Movie.




  • Jujutsu Kaisen

    Synopsis

    A young man by the name of Yuuji Itadori curses himself after ingesting one of 20 cursed fingers of Sukuna, an incredibly powerful Cursed Spirit transforming himself into Sukuna's host. He then enrolls in Tokyo Jujutsu High to find all the other 19 fingers and kill Sukuna who resides within him.

    Cast

    Junya Enoki, Yuuichi Nakamura, Yuuma Uchida, Asami Seto, Junichi Suwabe

    Director

    Sunghoo Park (Season 1) Shota Goshozono (Season 2)

    Duration

    2 Seasons, 47 Episodes, 24mins

    My Comments

    A thrilling series filled with action, death, and sorcery. If you enjoy paranormal and do not mind a bit of blood, this is for you! A story with interesting and well written characters both good and bad.

    9/10 Terrific series.





  • Mobile Suit Gundam: The Witch From Mercury

    Synopsis

    Suletta Mercury from planet Mercury transfers over to the Asticassia School of Technology run by a large conglomerate of business called the "Beneritt Group" which dominates the mobile suit industry. This is her story as she takes a step through this new world slowly making friends and enemies along the way.

    Cast

    Kana Ichinose, Mamiko Noto

    Director

    Yoshiyuki Tomino, Hajime Yatate

    Duration

    2 Seasons, 24 episodes, 24mins

    My Comments

    An action packed slice-of-life gundam anime. This anime is not only about new beginnings and action but discrimination between those born on Earth and those born in Space along with how corrupt businessmen (and women) can become.

    7.9/10 A simple series.
